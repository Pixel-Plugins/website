Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

PixelPlugins LLC ("Pixel Plugins," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information such as name, email address, and company details
  • Billing and payment information processed securely via third-party providers such as Stripe
  • Usage data such as browser type, device information, and interaction with our website or services
  • Support communications and messages you send to us

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions, licenses, or project-related services
  • To communicate with you and provide customer support
  • To improve our website, products, and user experience

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds: your consent, the need to perform a contract, compliance with legal obligations, or our legitimate business interests.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data
  • We may share data with trusted third-party service providers such as payment processors, hosting providers, analytics tools, or platforms used in connection with our services
  • These providers only process data as necessary to deliver their services to us or to you

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, measure performance, and analyze usage. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored in countries outside your own, depending on the location of our service providers and infrastructure.

9. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • You may request correction or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or contractual obligations
  • You may object to or request restriction of certain types of processing where applicable

10. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at support@pixelplugins.com.
