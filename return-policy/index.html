Return & Refund Policy

We do not offer refunds except in the following situations:

Refunds Are Only Issued If:

  • A critical bug prevents the software or plugin from working as promised, and we are unable to resolve it within a reasonable time
  • We fail to deliver agreed-upon features in a custom development project
  • There is a prolonged lapse in support or maintenance from our side that directly affects usability

Refunds Will Not Be Issued For:

  • Change of mind
  • Incorrect service selection
  • Lack of use after activation

Request Process:

All refund requests must be submitted in writing within 14 days of discovering the issue, with a clear explanation. We will review and decide at our discretion based on the nature of the request.
