BuildFire Plugin Maintenance

Keeping BuildFire plugins working through platform updates, dependency changes, and everyday bugs.

Plugins that worked fine at launch can break quietly as BuildFire updates the platform underneath them. We take on maintenance for existing plugins — including ones we didn't originally build — debugging, patching, and keeping them current.

What this covers

  • Debugging existing plugins, including ones built by another team
  • Upgrades to keep plugins working after BuildFire platform updates
  • Performance troubleshooting for plugins that have slowed down over time
  • iOS and Android testing after changes, not just a single-platform check
