DMNexa

An AI order assistant for social sellers, designed and built by PixelPlugins.

Sellers who run their business through Instagram conversations end up doing order entry by hand, reading back through messages to piece together what someone actually ordered. DMNexa turns that conversation directly into a clean, structured order.

What it does

  • Reads your Instagram inbox automatically
  • AI extracts order details from messy, unstructured conversations
  • Draft orders queued for seller review and confirmation before anything is final

How it works

  1. The seller chats with their customer like normal — no new workflow to learn
  2. DMNexa reads the conversation as it happens
  3. The seller reviews, adjusts, and confirms the draft order
  4. Every order ends up organized in one place

Where it runs

DMNexa is a cross-platform product — native apps on iOS and Android plus a web app — with subscription plans and a free trial for new sellers.

DMNexa is a live product, built and operated by PixelPlugins end to end. For the engineering side of how it was built, see our DMNexa case study.
