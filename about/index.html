About PixelPlugins

A product engineering company building scalable SaaS platforms, AI-powered operational systems, and custom extensions for platforms like BuildFire and Shopify.

PixelPlugins LLC is based in San Diego, California. We build custom SaaS platforms, AI-powered operational systems, and custom plugins and integrations for platforms including BuildFire and Shopify — and we build our own products too, including DMNexa.

How we work

We've run businesses across industries ourselves, so we advise like an owner, not a vendor billing by the hour. That shapes two things about how we deliver: we split roadmaps into phases you can validate before committing budget to the next one, and we size infrastructure to where your business actually is today rather than where a pitch deck says it might be.

What we build

  • Custom SaaS platforms and B2B software
  • AI agents and AI-assisted automation and data validation
  • Custom BuildFire and Shopify plugins and integrations
  • Platform modernization and migrations, including MongoDB
  • Compliance-ready systems for regulated industries — insurance, fintech, healthcare
