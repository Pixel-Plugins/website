Custom Software Development in San Diego

A San Diego-based product engineering team — not a remote agency with a mailing address.

PixelPlugins is based at 7670 Opportunity Rd Ste 255, San Diego, CA 92111. We're not a distributed team with a registered address of convenience — this is where the work actually happens, and we're available for in-person meetings by appointment for local clients who'd rather talk across a table than a video call.

Built here

DMNexa, our own AI order-assistant product, was designed and built by this team, in this office. It's live today and in active use by sellers turning Instagram and WhatsApp conversations into structured orders — not a prototype, a real product people run their business on.

Our history with BuildFire goes back to before PixelPlugins existed — our team worked as leading engineers on early BuildFire platform development. Since founding PixelPlugins, that's continued as custom plugin work out of this office, including an AI chatbot plugin and a HIPAA-ready dynamic form builder, both documented as public setup guides. See the details on our BuildFire work page.

The same is true of Flywheel — our team worked as leading engineers on early Flywheel platform development, before founding PixelPlugins.
