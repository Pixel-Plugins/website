What We Did

We built a private Shopify app for a retail brand to connect their in-store loyalty program with their online store.

The integration enables real-time point syncing between systems and allows customers to:

  • Link loyalty accounts via phone number
  • Earn points for online purchases
  • Redeem points for discounts directly at checkout
Slide 2 of 7

How It Works – Customer Side

  1. Step 1: Customer logs into their Shopify account.
  2. Step 2: On the account page, they can connect their in-store loyalty account using their phone number.
  3. Step 3: A 4-digit verification code is sent via SMS to securely link the account.
  4. Step 4: Once connected, customers can view available rewards and redeem them using their points.
  5. Step 5: When a reward is redeemed, a Shopify discount code is automatically generated and applied at checkout.
Slide 3 of 7

Admin Panel Features

The admin interface gives brand owners the ability to configure how customers redeem rewards. Admins can add and manage rewards through a dedicated rewards creation page.

Reward Creation Includes:

  • Reward Name: e.g. “$15 off entire order”
  • Reward Type: Percentage, Fixed Amount, or Free Shipping
  • Reward Value: e.g. 15%, $5, etc.
  • Applies To: Entire Order, Specific Collections, or Specific Products
  • Required Points: e.g. 300 points to redeem
